But even with so little, I gave everything to her.
She had debts — I cleared them.
Said she was hungry — I fed her.
Needed cab fare — I paid.
Gambling losses? — I covered them.
Then came more. She asked me to pay for her teeth, her dad’s pocket money, and even her flight expenses.
In just one week, I gave her $2,400.
And what did I get in return?
She brought men home. She cheated. She video called other guys while pretending to talk to me.
While I was out working, tired, pushing myself to get more money to help her — she was entertaining other men like I didn’t exist.
Then came the Kpods. I paid for three. She told me they were gone and gave me a half-used one. I didn’t even argue.
But later, I found a full Kpod hidden in her pants.
After all the sacrifices I made — even that, she chose to lie about.
She took everything until there was nothing left.
And now she dares to say life is unfair?
Retribution is coming.
She may not see it now, but karma doesn’t forget.
What she did wasn’t just selfish — it was heartless, calculated, and cold.
And karma always collects.
